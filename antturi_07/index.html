Eras

Film

8mm and 16mm are the beginning of skate filming, with early Southern California skate contests recorded on early hand held film cameras.

Tape

Filming suddenly becomes more accessible as cameras begin recording onto tape, allowing for easier processing and handling.

Digital

HD cameras come into the fold, changing skateboarding filming forever. Handy and small sd cards with large storage capabilites make recording easier and more accessible. Footage floods in.
